Our story

A family business built to last

Sand City has been a trusted name in Bloemfontein for over a decade. What started as a family venture has grown into one of the Free State's most reliable suppliers of sand and building materials.

We serve everyone — from large construction contractors laying foundations, to homeowners tackling weekend renovation projects. Quality materials, honest prices, and real service are what we stand for.
